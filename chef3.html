About me

Born into a family of artists and cooks in Ann Arbor, Michigan, I learned early on that food was a powerful medium for creation and change. I now cook and develop recipes in New York’s Hudson Valley, where I live with my husband Pizza Man and our rescue cat Kit-Kat
